# Pros and Cons of Alternative Data

## Pros

- One of the best sources of alpha available on the markets
  - Through access to private and unique datasets you have information that no other trader has
  - Combining multiple datasets to create a new dataset can provide a huge trading advantage

## Cons

- Requires lots of capital to purchase data
- Very resource intensive to clean, organize, and manipulate data
- Data can be inaccurate which is a difficult issue to recognize
- Accurately understanding the data is far from easily
  - Read up on (Zillow's failed house trading algorithm)[https://www.geekwire.com/2021/ibuying-algorithms-failed-zillow-says-business-worlds-love-affair-ai/amp/]
