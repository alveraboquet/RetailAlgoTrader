## TradingView Integration

The following lists are all brokers that integrate with TradingView. TradingView is the best charting software out there and even
includes its own coding langauge Pinescript. Pinescript allows you to create custom indicators and trading robots. If you are
interested more in manual trading than algorithmic trading at this point in time you should choose one of the below brokers.

The different tiers of brokers are published by TradingView. We recommend you focus on the Platinum and Gold brokers; however,
any of them should be fine.
